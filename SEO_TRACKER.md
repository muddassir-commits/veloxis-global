## 🎯 Step-by-Step On-Page SEO Audit Checklist
For *each* page in this checklist, verify the following 5 crucial on-page criteria:

1. **Meta Title (40-70 chars)**: Ensure the target keyword is placed at the beginning, followed by the brand identifier `| Veloxis Global`.
2. **Meta Description (100-170 chars)**: Summarize the page in simple English, including the target keyword and a strong call-to-action (e.g. *"Get your free audit today"*).
3. **Heading Hierarchy**: Exactly one `H1` tag containing the primary keyword, followed by logically ordered `H2` and `H3` subheadings.
4. **Structured Schema**: Confirm that layout schemas (Breadcrumb, LocalBusiness, FAQ, Service, or Article) render correctly without warnings.
5. **Interactive Element Accessibility**: Ensure all clickable elements (Buttons, Inputs) have unique, descriptive `id` attributes.
